Spaghetti with pilchards

This dish contains I packet of spaghetti and a can of pilchards. The dish itelf is very high in nutrients and also high in fibre.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Pilchards

Method

Cook the spaghetti in boiling water until it is soft.

Lower the heat and let it cook until the water level has lowered or decreased to your liking.

Heat up the pilchards in a frying pan. You can add any extra spices or even onions. It is up to you.

Mix the contents of the pan (the pilchards) with the speghetti

Stir until it is mixed in well.

Feel free to add in extra vegetables of your choice.

And there you have it, a well served meal that should take you less than an hour to prepare.
